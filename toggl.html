Toggl Email Generator
Interaction Design

Project Brief

I was tasked to help Toggl, the time tracking software, to design a simple landing page with an email generator. The idea was to create a landing page that showcased the main text while adding a touch of light-hearted fun. After all, the landing page’s intention was to generate funny emails to freelancers who get asked to do things for free or for very little money.

Design

Toggl already had a very clear vision of the elements they needed in the page so I was there mostly to stylize it according to the vibe they wanted to emote. I took inspirations from the soft yet vibrant colours from Toggl’s branding direction and included a hero illustration to amp up the delivery of the page’s message. .
